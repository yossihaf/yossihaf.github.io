Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize this application, we receive a TikTok access token that allows us to publish videos on your behalf. We do not collect personal information beyond what TikTok provides through their API (basic profile info and publishing permissions).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your TikTok authorization is used solely to publish video content that you initiate. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored securely and used only for the authorized purpose. We do not store your TikTok password or any content you have not explicitly submitted for publishing.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Data Retention

You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings. Upon revocation, we delete all stored tokens associated with your account.

6. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with TikTok's API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
